#
# converts notes into major triads from C3-B3
#

OnNote Any NoteC3 Any
	Print "Hello World" Velocity "What's up"
	SendNote Channel NoteC3 Velocity
	SendNote Channel NoteE3 Velocity
	SendNote Channel NoteG3 Velocity
End

	SendNote Channel NoteDb3 Velocity
	SendNote Channel NoteF3 Velocity

End

End
